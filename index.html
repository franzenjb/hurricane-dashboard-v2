Data Verification Process

Multi-Source Verification

Every storm entry undergoes rigorous cross-referencing between official NHC reports, contemporary newspaper accounts, ship logs, and weather station data. Discrepancies are resolved using the most reliable sources available for each era.

Historical Era Classification

Satellite Era (1980-2024): High-precision satellite tracking, microwave imagery, scatterometer data

Aircraft Era (1944-1979): Hurricane hunter flights, dropsonde measurements, radar observations

Weather Bureau Era (1900-1943): Professional weather stations, telegraph reports, barometer networks

Early Records Era (1851-1899): Ship logs, lighthouse records, colonial weather diaries

Project Information

This dashboard provides comprehensive hurricane landfall data for Florida from 1851-2024, supporting disaster preparedness, historical research, and educational initiatives. All data is verified against official NOAA/NHC records.
